What's the PTO accrual rate for new employees?

New hires accrue 10 days per year, added monthly.

That rate applies to employees with 0–2 years of service. A few related rules:

  • Planned absences need a request at least two weeks ahead.1
  • Sick or emergency leave needs no notice — just tell your supervisor by the start of your shift.
  • Up to 5 unused days carry into the next year; the rest are forfeited on January 1.2
